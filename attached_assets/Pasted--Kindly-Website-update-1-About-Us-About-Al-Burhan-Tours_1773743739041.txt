1️⃣ About Us
About Al Burhan Tours & Travels
Al Burhan Tours & Travels is a trusted travel service provider based in Burhanpur, Madhya Pradesh, India. We specialize in organizing Hajj, Umrah, and Ziyarat pilgrimage tours for pilgrims across India. Our goal is to provide comfortable, reliable, and spiritually fulfilling travel experiences for our customers.
With years of experience in pilgrimage travel services, we offer complete assistance including visa processing, air ticketing, hotel accommodation, transportation, and guided religious tours in Saudi Arabia and other Islamic heritage destinations.
We also organize Ziyarat tours to historical and religious destinations including Iraq, Syria, Jordan, and Baitul Muqaddas (Jerusalem) where possible according to travel regulations.
Our dedicated team works closely with pilgrims to ensure smooth travel arrangements, quality services, and continuous support throughout the journey.

4️⃣ Cancellation Policy
Cancellation Policy – Al Burhan Tours & Travels
All cancellations must be made in writing via email or through our office.
Cancellation charges may apply depending on the time of cancellation before the travel date.
General cancellation guidelines:
* More than 60 days before departure – minimal administrative charges may apply.
* 30 to 60 days before departure – partial cancellation fee may apply.
* Less than 30 days before departure – higher cancellation charges may apply.
* No-show or last-minute cancellation – full package amount may be non-refundable.
Visa processing fees, airline charges, and hotel booking fees may be non-refundable depending on service provider policies.

5️⃣ Refund Policy
Refund Policy – Al Burhan Tours & Travels
Refunds will be processed according to the cancellation policy and service provider conditions.
Refunds may be issued in the following cases:

Refund processing may take 7 to 21 business days depending on banking and payment gateway processing.

Refunds will be issued using the original payment method where possible.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
1. What services does Al Burhan Tours & Travels provide?
We provide complete travel services including Hajj packages, Umrah packages, Ziyarat tours, visa processing, air ticket booking, hotel accommodation, and transportation services.

2. How can I book a Hajj or Umrah package?
You can book your package by contacting our office directly, through WhatsApp, or by submitting a booking request on our website.

3. What documents are required for Umrah or Hajj travel?
Generally the required documents include:
* Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)
* Passport size photographs
* Identity proof
* Vaccination certificate (if required)
* Other documents depending on travel regulations
Our team will guide you through the full process.

4. Do you provide visa services?
Yes, we assist customers with Umrah visa processing and travel documentation as part of our travel packages.

5. Do you arrange group travel for Hajj and Umrah?
Yes, we organize group pilgrimages with experienced guides to ensure a comfortable and organized travel experience.

6. Can I pay in installments?
In many cases, customers can pay in installments according to booking terms. Please contact our office for details.

7. Do you organize Ziyarat tours?
Yes, we organize religious Ziyarat tours to historical Islamic sites including destinations in Saudi Arabia, Iraq, Jordan, and other locations depending on travel regulations.
