Non-Refundable Items

  • Visa processing fees (once applied)
  • Airline ticket cancellation charges (as per airline policy)
  • Insurance premiums
  • Any government-imposed charges

Visa Rejection

In case of visa rejection by Saudi authorities, we will refund the package cost minus:

  • Visa application fees
  • Any non-refundable hotel/airline deposits
  • Service charge of ₹2,000 per person

Refund Process

  • Approved refunds will be processed within 7–14 working days
  • Refunds for online payments will be returned to the original payment method
  • For cash/bank transfer payments, refunds will be made via NEFT/IMPS

Cancellation by Al Burhan Tours

If Al Burhan Tours & Travels cancels your booking due to circumstances beyond our control, we will provide a full refund of all amounts paid, processed within 7 working days.
