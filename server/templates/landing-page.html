About Us

Your Trusted Partner for the Journey of a Lifetime

Al Burhan Tours & Travels has been guiding pilgrims from Burhanpur and surrounding regions on their sacred journeys to Makkah and Madinah for over 15 years. We combine deep Islamic knowledge with professional travel expertise to ensure every pilgrim's experience is comfortable, spiritual, and memorable.

  • Expert scholarship-backed religious guidance throughout the journey
  • Premium hotels close to Haram in both Makkah & Madinah
  • Complete visa, documentation & medical assistance
  • Complimentary Ihram, travel kit & Zam-Zam water (Hajj packages)
  • Air-conditioned transport & 24/7 support on ground
  • Flexible payment installments to suit every family
