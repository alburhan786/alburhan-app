Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Al Burhan Tours & Travels ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Al Burhan Tours mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information:

  • Identity information: Full name, date of birth, nationality
  • Contact information: Phone number, email address
  • Travel documents: Passport number, passport copy, visa details
  • Payment information: Payment history and transaction IDs (we do not store card details)
  • Booking information: Package selections, travel dates, number of travelers

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and confirm your Hajj/Umrah booking
  • To communicate booking updates via SMS and WhatsApp
  • To process payments securely through Razorpay
  • To comply with visa and government requirements
  • To send invoices and travel documents
  • To improve our services

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Saudi Arabian visa authorities for Hajj/Umrah visa processing
  • Hotels and transport providers for accommodation and travel arrangements
  • Payment processors (Razorpay) for secure payment handling
  • SMS/WhatsApp service providers for communication

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. Your payment details are processed by Razorpay and are never stored on our servers.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill your booking and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time.

6. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
